Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a breathing session?

Open the app, tap "Start session" on the Today screen, choose your duration and ambient sound, then tap "Begin."

Is the app free?

The 3-week Foundation journey (nasal, belly, and 3D breathing) is completely free. Premium unlocks all 9 techniques, complete journeys, and ambient sounds. Premium includes a 7-day free trial.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find Breathwise and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the "You" tab in the app, scroll down to "Restore purchases" and tap it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the "You" tab, scroll to the Account section, and tap "Delete account and all data." This permanently removes all your data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All breathing techniques, journeys, and ambient sounds work without an internet connection.
